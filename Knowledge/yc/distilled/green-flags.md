# YC Distilled: Green Flags And Strong Signals

## Purpose

This file consolidates the most positive recurring YC-style signals in the current knowledge dump.

## Founder Green Flags

- Founder experienced the problem personally.
- Founder has deep domain expertise.
- At least one founder can build immediately.
- Co-founders have worked together before.
- Team is full-time and already shipping.
- Founders are relentlessly resourceful.

## Problem And Idea Green Flags

- Hair-on-fire problem.
- Users already use ugly workarounds.
- Idea comes from lived experience or sharp insight.
- There is obvious pain plus a believable 10x improvement.
- The opportunity includes schlep that deters weaker competitors.

## Market And Timing Green Flags

- Small wedge with large upside.
- Growing market or strong why-now.
- Competition proves demand, while the team sees something incumbents missed.
- Market seems underestimated by outsiders.

## Traction And Validation Green Flags

- Paying users early.
- Strong retention or flattening cohorts.
- 5-7% WoW growth is good; 10% WoW is exceptional.
- Founders know core metrics and bottlenecks.
- Users adopt rough versions because the pain is real.

## Interview And Application Green Flags

- Clear one-sentence explanation.
- Progress between application and interview.
- Honest, concise, and direct answers.
- All founders contribute.
- Strong answer to "why you, why this, why now?"
