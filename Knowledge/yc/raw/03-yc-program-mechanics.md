# YC Knowledge Base: YC Program Mechanics & Culture
*Source: "What Happens at YC" Overview*

## 1. The Funding Standard
- **Total Investment:** $500,000.
- **The $125k Safe:** Post-money safe for 7% of the company.
- **The $375k MFN Safe:** Uncapped safe with a "Most Favored Nation" provision (matches the terms of the next best investor).

## 2. Program Structure
- **Intensity:** 11 weeks of "all startup, all the time" leading to Demo Day.
- **Groups and Sections:** Startups are split into small sections (6-10 companies) for intimate partner advising.
- **Office Hours:** Group sessions every two weeks + unlimited 1-on-1s.

## 3. The Ecosystem
- **Bookface:** A private platform for knowledge sharing, expert tagging, and internal networking.
- **Alumni Talks:** Weekly off-the-record stories from successful founders (Airbnb, Stripe) to show the "unfiltered" truth of early days.
- **First Customers:** YC B2B companies often get their first 40–50 customers directly from the batch.

## 4. Demo Day and Beyond
- **The Event:** A high-pressure presentation to selected investors and press.
- **Price Competition:** YC creates an environment where investors compete, often leading to higher valuations.
- **Post-Batch:** Access to office hours and the 6,000+ member alumni network never expires.

## 5. Core Values
- **Relentless Motivation:** Creating the right conditions to bring out potential founders didn't know they had.
- **Helpfulness:** A culture where alumni are committed to helping new batches.
- **Credibility:** The YC brand provides a "look" from investors/press for first-time founders.
